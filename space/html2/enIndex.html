ABOUT

Our headquarters are located in Fukushima - a name that has become known worldwide as a result of the 2011 Japan Earthquake. We also have a branch office in Tokyo.

Japan has many wonderful traditions, culture, nature, and technologies, and is home to many sincere consumers who are open to new ideas. We believe that more people around the world should get to know Japan, so we aim to support companies that want to challenge the Japanese market and expand Japanese businesses globally.
